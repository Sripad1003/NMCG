OUR MISSION

Together, We're Dedicated to Saving the Ganga

At NMCG, our dedicated commitment extends to the preservation and restoration of the Ganga River, a cultural and environmental treasure. We denounce any harm to this sacred river and work tirelessly to address its challenges. Our mission involves uniting communities, fostering awareness, and mobilizing collective action for the Ganga's sustainable future. Through strategic initiatives and community engagement, we aim to cleanse the Ganga and empower communities as stewards. Fostering shared responsibility, we aspire to rejuvenate the Ganga, making it a symbol of environmental resilience and communal harmony.
